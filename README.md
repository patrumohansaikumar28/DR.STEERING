# 🚗 DR. STEERING AI

**DR. STEERING AI** is an AI-powered browser racing game that uses **real-time hand gesture recognition** to control a vehicle without a keyboard or mouse. Built with **HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, Canvas API, and MediaPipe Hands**, the game transforms your webcam into a gesture-based steering controller, creating an interactive and immersive driving experience.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🤖 AI-powered hand gesture recognition
- ✋ Finger tracking using MediaPipe Hands
- 🚗 Gesture-based car steering
- 🎮 Endless racing gameplay
- 🚧 Randomly generated traffic obstacles
- 📈 Progressive difficulty system
- 🏆 High score saved using Local Storage
- 🎵 Dynamic retro-style background music
- 📹 Live webcam preview with gesture tracking
- 🌐 Runs directly in the browser

## 🎮 Gameplay

Control your car using only your **index finger**.

### Controls

- ☝️ Raise your index finger to activate steering.
- 👈 Move your finger left to steer left.
- 👉 Move your finger right to steer right.
- 🚗 Avoid incoming traffic vehicles.
- 🏆 Earn points by surviving longer.
- 💥 Colliding with another vehicle ends the game.
